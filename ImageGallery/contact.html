Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share my visual vibes on VisualVibe?

Simply create your account, click "Upload" or "Share Your Vibes", and drag & drop your images or browse to select them. We support JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats up to 15MB each.

Is VisualVibe free to use?

Yes! VisualVibe offers a free tier that includes unlimited uploads, community features, and access to our creative tools. We also offer premium plans with additional storage and advanced features.

Can I monetize my visual vibes?

We're currently developing our Creator Program that will allow artists to monetize their work through various channels. Join our community to be the first to know when it launches!

How do I report inappropriate content?

You can report any content by clicking the flag icon on any image or user profile. Our community team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action to maintain positive vibes.

Can I download and share other creators' vibes?

Yes, you can share and download images that creators have made available. Always respect the creator's licensing terms and give proper attribution when sharing their vibes.
